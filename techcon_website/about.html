History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts, and has since grown into one of the most influential tech conferences in the world. Every year, we bring together industry leaders, developers, and innovators to discuss the latest trends in technology and showcase groundbreaking solutions.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower tech professionals by providing them with a platform to share ideas, network, and discover the latest innovations. At TechCon, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives, businesses, and communities for the better.

Past Speakers

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, was a keynote speaker at TechCon 2018. He shared his insights on AI, machine learning, and the future of digital information.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Meta, spoke at TechCon 2020 about leadership in tech and the role of women in technology. Her powerful message resonated with audiences worldwide.
